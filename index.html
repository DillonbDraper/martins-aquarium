Tips on keeping your fish (and tank) healthy
  • Remember to keep tank oxygenation and salination levels static.
  • Conditioning the water. Tap water comes with countless properties that need to be balanced in an aquarium to support aquatic life. To condition water properly, use a dechlorinating agent along with a biological aquarium supplement.
  • Always use coated or prewashed gravel. NEVER use regular gravel in a fish tank.
  • In the interest of saving on prewashed or coated gravel, you can also use a gravel cleaner.
  • Use a quality net that you are comfortable with when transferring fish
